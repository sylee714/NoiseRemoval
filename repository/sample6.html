BroncoDirect/PeopleSoft

Which one do I use?

The one used by most is BroncoDirect/PeopleSoft For Student System. This is where applicants, students, faculty, and advisors access all of the features related to Student Administration, including, but not limited to, enrollment, fee payment, financial aid, and application status.

BroncoDirect/PeopleSoft For HR is for use by employees to enter absences, check sick/vacation balances, and update their personal information.

Log in using your BroncoName and password. Once you are logged in, use the menu to locate the services you are seeking.
